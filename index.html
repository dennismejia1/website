About

Dennis Mejia is currently a junior at Molloy College in Rockville Centre, New York where he is studying in the New Media field. Recently switching his field of study from biology, he looks to understand and perfect the different aspects that are in the world of new media. He plans to take all the experience he has learned to be able to help out propective high school athletes in the recruiting process by making recruiting videos for them. The main goal would be for coaches at collegiate, amatuer, and professional levels to view the video and assess the athlete that can potentially be a good fit for their program.
